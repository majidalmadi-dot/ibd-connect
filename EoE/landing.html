Everything for living with EoE
Built around eosinophilic esophagitis

Not a generic tracker — every screen is shaped by what matters in EoE: swallowing, triggers, diet therapy and treatment.

😣

Track swallowing

Log dysphagia by food type, food-impaction events, reflux, and the eating tactics you use to get food down.

📊

Your scores

An EEsAI-style symptom index, a DSQ daily diary, the EREFS endoscopy radar, and your biopsy eosinophil count vs the 15/hpf line.

🥗

Diet & triggers

Follow elimination diets (six-food, step-up, elemental), log meals, and let the trigger finder surface food–symptom patterns.

💊

Treatment, simply

Record PPIs, swallowed steroids (with the “no food 30 min” reminder) and dupilumab — only what your doctor prescribed.

📚

Learn EoE

A bilingual library: what EoE is, diagnosis, the 3 D’s, elimination diets, food-impaction first aid, and living well.

📤

Share with your doctor

Generate a clean, read-only summary of your scores and recent logs to make every clinic visit count.

🎨

Make it yours

Personalize with 20+ illustrated themes & wallpapers — anime, nature, seasonal, retro and calm — plus a signature accent colour you choose.

☁️

Private cloud backup

Optional end-to-end encrypted backup with a recovery code only you hold — restore on a new device anytime. Neither we nor the server can read it.

Maeen is a non-medical health companion for personal tracking and education about eosinophilic esophagitis. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment and is not a substitute for professional care. If food is stuck and you cannot swallow your saliva, or you have chest pain or trouble breathing, seek urgent care. Always consult your healthcare provider.
